NEOHUMANISM

From A'nanda Ma'rga Philosophy


When "One" creates many that One is called the Noumenal Cause. And when “many” is created from One, that “many” is called the Phenomenal Effect.
This phenomenal world has come out of Noumenal cause.

Every created being is associated with its own dharma. Dharma is an inherent characteristic on which all other characteristics depend. For example, the innate characteristic of fire is to burn whatever it meets. So the dharma of fire is 'to burn'.

Human being is the highest of His creations. The innate characteristic of human being is to be one with the Noumenal cause that means he/she has to return the original abode. So dharma of human being is the Bhagawata Dharma - “the way of life which leads to ensconcement in the supreme stance”.

Returning home needs three things:

  • Shravana (hearing about the Supreme): This enables one to cross the vibrational faculty(ocean of bha'va).
  • Manana (ideating on the Supreme): This enables one to cross the premordial faculty.
  • Nidi-Dhya'sana (meditating on the Supreme with an unbroken flow of mind): This enables one to merge with the Supreme entity.
