NEOHUMANISM
Ten Keys of Neo-humanist Awareness
  1. Life is sacred. Nothing is unimpotant. We must learn to utilize all resources for individual and collective welfare.
  2. Humans must always maintain a sense of gratitude and humility to the highest consciouness as they interact in this world.
  3. Everything is interconnected. The existence of both animate and inanimate is tied together through a fragile thread of interdependence.
  4. Inner ecology and outer ecology are two faces of one life force. Both need our careful nurturing.
  5. Our thoughts, word and deeds must be in harmony and be always guided by sublime motives.
  6. We are all different, guided by our unique samskaras (reactive momenta). But all samskaras also emerge from Cosmic will. So there is no reason to panic or be hopeless or feel helpless. Surrenderto the Supreme(devotion) should be our ultimate solice.
  7. We cannot protect what we donot understad. So, we must always keep alive our thirst for knowledge with a positive attitude towards life and learning.
  8. Scintific temprament is an antidote to dogmatic faith. We must rise above all sectarian feeling and preseve 'universalism' as the core value of life.
  9. Fear and love will never co-exist. We must transform fearfulness and into fearless love.
  10. Synthesis of head (reasoning), heart(love), hands (action) and legs (mobility) essential. We must learn to live a dynamic life free of physical violence and solve all conflicts inelligently and with a magnanimous heart.
