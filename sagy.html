NEOHUMANISM

Dada Shambhushivananda ji chaired the session on education and gave his keynote adress concerning the "Sansad Adarsh Gram Yojna(SAGY)"- A dream project of Narendra Modi, Prime Minister of India. The two day event, Oct. 29-30th 2017, marked the beginning of a national movement to link up higher educational institutions with 600,000 villages of India. It is indeed a laudable initiative of AICTE which is now actively being promoted by IECSME, Village Parishads in India.
