Wisdom Quotient

WQ = IQ + PQ + SQ

WQ = Wisdom Quotient
IQ = Intelligence Quotient
PQ = Productivity Quotient
SQ = Spiritual Quotient

Neo-humanist education aims to develop the Wisdom Quotient in the society which begins with rationality and culminates in higher spiritual awareness.
